Planigrupo: Creating Value
We are a Mexican company leader in the development, design, construction, marketing and administration of shopping centers nationwide.
Focus
Our main focus is to create added value for our investors, partners, clients and users through a portfolio of properties that offers an invaluable offer with the highest quality standards.
Experience
We have over 47 years of experience changing the way shopping centers are seen, designed, and developed.
Planigrupo by the Numbers
With more than 1,200 tenants distributed in approximately 814,000 m2 of profitable area (GLA) in its managed shopping centers, Planigrupo in 2021 received more than 99.1 million visitors.
